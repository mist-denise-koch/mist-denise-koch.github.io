How to Coordinate Team Meetings Using Google Calendar



Solutions for Team Collaboration

It can be difficult to coordinate team meetings when everyone has different time commitments. Miscommunication can tangle the best laid plans of an Instructional Design Team so having group meetings can help to curtail confusion. Before you schedule a meeting, you can check everyone’s availability in Google Calendar, which is part of Google Workspace.

Here are some easy steps and tips to help your team coordinate meeting times that work for everyone.




Image Reference


Step 1: Open Google Calendar and click "Create"

In some versions the "Create" button is in the upper left of the calendar. It sometimes displays as a Google "plus icon"



Google Icons Reference



Step 2: Select the date and time range

You can drag the pointer to include available blocks of time.




Google Icons Reference



Step 3: Select "Add Guests"

You will now be able to share a calendar with specific people. People can be selected within your organization that are listed by email.

(Tip: To share a calendar that you don’t own, you need to ask the owner to give you “Make changes and manage sharing” permission.)


Image Credit


Step 4: Don't forget to hit "Save" when you're done so your guests (team members) will receive the available options

error If you forget to hit save, your team will not be notified by email.



Image Credit


Step 5: After saving, you will then be prompted to hit "Send" to notify your team of the available time slots

There are many scheduling tools that can be of assistance.

Google Calendar is just one tool that offers many great features Instructional Designers have found helpful to improve team collaboration. For a comprehensive overview of how to best utilize all that Google Calendar has to offer take time to visit this Google Calendar Tutorial Video.

Since there are so many features available, it would be helpful for the team to take some time and get familiar with these features.




Step 6: Keep in mind the advantages of utilizing scheduling tools to organize your team

Some advantages of using Google Calendar as a scheduling application include:

  1. Organizes appointments
  2. Provides direct access to virtual Google Meet Video Conferencing
  3. Allows you to view Google Tasks assigned to you
